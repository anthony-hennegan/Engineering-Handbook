# Engineering Principals

**Lesson:** 000  
**Category:** Foundations  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Last Updated:** 2026-06-23

Ideas to always keep in mind throughout the build of every project. 

## Principal 1 - Understand Before Memorizing
Learn why something works beofre to remember how to write it.

---

## Principal 2 - Consistency Beats Intensity
One focused hour every day is better than one marathon session each month.

---

## Principal 3 - Build Systems, Not Shortcuts
Create workflows, notes, and habits that make future work easier.

---

## Principal 4 - One Commit, One Logical Change
Every commit should represent one meaningful improvement.

---

## Principal 5 - Documentation Is Part of the Project
A project is not complete without clear documentation.

---

## Principle 6 - Make Mistakes Safely
Use Git, testing, and experimentation to learn without fear.

---

## Principle 7 - Code for Humans First
Readable code is more valuable than clever code.

---

## Principle 8 - Practice Deliberately
Identify weaknesses and train them through repetition.

---

## Principle 10 - Never Stop Learning
Technology changes constantly. Strong engineers remain curious and continue learning throughout their careers
